I build products, not just code.

I started where most developers do staring at a blank HTML file, figuring out what a <div> is. But I didn't stop there. Within months I was writing JavaScript, then React, then TypeScript. Today I work across the full stack — web, mobile, and backend and I lead engineering teams building real products used by real people.

I'm the Founder of Eukora, an EdTech startup where I'm designing and building an AI-powered learning platform from the ground up. Simultaneously, I serve as Head of Engineering at Chopa, where I architect systems, lead the dev team, and translate business requirements into shipped software.

What sets me apart is how I work. I'm a hybrid engineer — I combine traditional manual coding when precision matters, AI-assisted development for rapid iteration and intelligent problem-solving, and vibe coding when the goal is to move fast, explore ideas, and ship. I'm also a Prompt Engineer — I understand how to communicate with AI systems to get production-quality results, not just demos.

My background spans work at Excuxion and a technical internship at Ghana Water Company. I don't just write code — I own products, solve problems, and ship.
